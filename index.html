Health, Safety & Environment (HSE)

Our HSE plan ensures maximum safety for people and property, with minimal environmental impact.

  • Daily safety meetings & toolbox talks
  • PPE compliance & work‑at‑height controls
  • Permit‑to‑work: Hot Work, Excavation, Height
  • Clear site signage & disciplined housekeeping
  • Emergency procedures & head‑count accountability
  • LEED experience & Green Building compliance
  • Responsible waste handling & disposal

Site Safety Rules

  1. Always wear PPE on site
  2. Follow signages & supervisor instructions
  3. Maintain tidy work areas; remove hazards
  4. Isolate energy sources; verify permits
  5. Report incidents; participate in training
